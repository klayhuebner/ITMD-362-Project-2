The Region's Premier Convention For Japanese Pop Culture, Fashion, Anime, Music, and Gaming

Voice Actors

Matt Mercer

Matthew Mercer is a Los Angeles based actor/voice actor known for his roles in animation and video games.

Marisha Ray

Marisha Ray (Actor, Host, Writer, Producer) has often been described as a geek girl, game enthusiast, uber-nerd and pretty much any other variant.

Micah Solusod

Despite his general inability to fly, Micah Solusod is a professional voice actor and illustrator.

Musical Guests

DJ BASS

In 1990, as a local DJ he formed the legendary rap group ZINGI. Later he broke out into his own as a popular DJ working with local musical artists on a wide range of activities. His specialty musical genres include rap and hip hop.

BACK-ON

Taking a break from their world tour, BACK-ON from Japan is the band for Tekko 2016! BACK-ON, a 4 member hybrid rock band, was formed in Japan in 2002 and made their major label debut in 2005. They have released 15 singles and 5 albums so far.

Industry Guests

Charles Dunbar

Charles Dunbar is an anthropologist. He studies a lot of things, be it fandom, anime culture, the supernatural or mysterious men in blue boxes.

Uncle Yo

From Otakon to Anime Expo, Karl “Uncle Yo” Custer teleports to anime, comic book and video game conventions performing his unique brand of geek-specific stand-up comedy.

Nancy Kepner

Born and raised in Pittsburgh, Nancy Kepner writes comedic songs about Otaku and Geek culture. When performed at family reunions, Nancy Kepner’s songs are never a hit.

Fashion Guests

Misako Aoki

Misako Aoki was appointed a “Kawaii Ambassador” (Lolita fashion representative) by the Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs in 2009, she has visited with many fans overseas at events in over 20 countries and 30 cities!

Lynda Leung

Joining us this year is Lynda of Saxony Company, a founding executive of Angelic Pretty USA. She is best known for introducing and representing popular J-fashion labels and fashionable talents to America.
